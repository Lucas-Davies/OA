About Jody Graham

Jody Graham is a Sydney-based multidisciplinary artist whose practice spans drawing, sculpture, mixed media, installation and performance. Known for using unconventional mark-making tools, she explores gesture, texture and the physical act of drawing.

She has taught art for more than two decades through art schools, workshops and community programs, helping students of all levels explore creativity and confidence in their work. In 2022 she won the prestigious Adelaide Perry Prize for Drawing and continues to lead courses and exhibitions across Australia.
